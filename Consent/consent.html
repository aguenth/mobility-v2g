Consent Form


This is a follow-up questionnaire on a survey that you took last year in September. The survey last year was about your charging preferences for your electric vehicle and vehicle-to-grid technology.

This study is supervised by Prof. Ulf J.J. Hahnel and MSc Anne Günther, Faculty of Psychology, University of Basel, Switzerland.

If you agree to be in this study, you will be asked to complete a series of questions about your driving behavior. Participation in this study will involve no more than 10 minutes. There are no known risks associated with your participation in this research beyond those of everyday life. You will be payed by the market research company for completing this study. This research may help the investigators understand your mobility behavior.

Confidentiality of your research records will be strictly maintained by assigning code numbers to each participant so that data is never directly linked to individual identity. The data will be made available in anonymized form on Open Science platforms allowing other researchers to use the data for research purposes without revealing your identity.

Participation in this study is voluntary. You may refuse to participate or withdraw at any time without penalty.

If there is anything about the study or your participation that is unclear or that you do not understand, if you have questions or wish to report a research-related problem, you may contact MSc Anne Günther, anne.guenther@unibas.ch.
